Features

Set of features included in the study Bible.

Wide margin for notes taking

In the Bible we typeset, we leave a margin on each page for your notes. There are two column: one with the Bible verses, the other with space so you can write there. You can print each chapter and write with a pencil or directly take notes with a stylus on the PDF page.

Contextual information

We gather some contextual information like cross-references on each page, related to a verse. During your study, we can refer to it in order to enrich your knowledge. We will also typeset maps and other useful information with the verses to ease your study.

Summary sheet for reminders

A summary sheet is a page where we lease space and some intructions to write key points of your readings. The sheet is to help you memorise the Bible chapters you read. At the end of the book, you can reuse this chapter key points as milestones to reminder the stories, events, characters or teachings..
