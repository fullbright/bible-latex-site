About

They donate their time, skills and passion to give life to this project.

  • Early 2018

    Our Humble Beginnings

    This project starts in the morning prayer, a time set apart to listen to the Lord Jesus and speak to him. As it grows, the idea of the Bible study by writing next to the verses became more persistant.We did some research and typeset the first Bible!

  • April 2018

    We typeset the first Bible

    We typeset the first Bible using Latex. It was a KJV, in English. But shortly, we needed to have a Bible in French, so we looked and found a LSG Bible in French.

  • June 2018

    Transition to a French Bible

    Our main European language is French. To test the study experience, we needed a Bible in French, with the margin to see how we can study and how efficient this model is. We were amazed how much we learn.

  • July 2014

    Adding more material in the margin

    We noticed that more material is needed to have an in depth study. We gathered some references and worked to add them to the typeset Bible. And the story goes on, as we discover more pattern in the Bible, as we continue studying...
